17 West Lawn

The first official meeting of Trigon Engineering Society, originally named the Delta Society, was held in Mr. Woods Stockton Walker’s room, 17 West Lawn, in 1924. Since then, over 50 Trigon brothers have been selected as the resident for Room 17 West Lawn.
